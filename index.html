Tap the NHS App

The NHS App helps people in our community get more out of their healthcare. It gives you a safe and simple way to cancel doctor appointments, order medicines, look at your health records, and check your test results all in one place.

Learn more about the NHS App See upcoming support sessions

What patients can do

The NHS App helps patients stay in control of routine care and access important information without unnecessary delay.

  • Manage appointments
  • Request repeat prescriptions
  • See your health records
  • Check test results
